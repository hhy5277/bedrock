Private Browser

No chance for Cookies and Co. Block third party trackers and website elements, that want to track your activities on the web. You can decide if you want to block those always, only in private mode or user-defined.

Device Sync

Everything under control, on every device. With your Firefox account you can sync bookmarks, send open tab to another device with one click, safely access your passwords, safe notes and much more.

Forget about remembering all the passwords. Log in with your Firefox account – your passwords and logins are already waiting for you there – on all your devices. Easy and safe.
